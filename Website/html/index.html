This tool uses MITRE's documented Attack Techniques and associated Mitigations to provide an alternative Risk Assessment tool that can be conducted on Enterprise or Mobile Infrastructures. In this process, you will be guided through 3 sections: Filtering of Techniques, the Mitigations associated with these filtered Techniques and finally view a Matrix overview of your Domain's defensive coverage.
