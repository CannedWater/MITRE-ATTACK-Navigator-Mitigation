Tolerance Explanation

Tolerance Level is used to define your Risk Appetite that will affect how the Risk Score for a technique is calculated. The default selection is Medium but if you are Risk Averse it is recommended to select a Low Tolerance Level. In the case of being open to Risk, you are able to represent this by selecting a High Tolerance Level.
